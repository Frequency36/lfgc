  • The concert will begin with a brief narration of the Old Testament prophecies about the coming of the Messiah. It will also be accompanied by music by the choir, setting a sacred and expectant tone.
  • Scripture reading: Isaiah 9:6 (NIV)
    “For to us a child is born, to us a son is given, and the government will be on his shoulders. And he will be called Wonderful Counselor, Mighty God, Everlasting Father, Prince of Peace.”
  • Music: “O Come, O Come Emmanuel,” This will be performed with a world-music flair to represent the anticipation of the Saviour across the globe. It would be performed by a group of students from the different regions and involve different instruments from around the world (we can improvise with African drums, guitars, etc)
